Opening Protocol (First 3 minutes)

1 ACKNOWLEDGE: "Welcome everyone here in the room AND online"
2 ESTABLISH: How to participate (multiple ways to show presence)
3 VERIFY: "Remote folks, confirm you can see the slide"
4 CONNECT: Use names from both modalities immediately
5 PRESENCE PACT: "Remember, we all find ways to show up"
Try card A1 - Presence Signaling for verbal confirmation patterns
Reference - The Participant Guide students received about presence

Quick Fixes for Common Challenges

Remote Students Silent/Invisible

Understanding the Dynamic:

  • Students may have legitimate constraints
  • Community needs presence signals
  • Balance is negotiated, not mandated
Warning Signs:
  • No chat activity for 10+ minutes
  • All cameras off
  • No voluntary responses
  • Names rarely mentioned

Quick Fixes:

ActionSay ThisCard to Try
Acknowledge constraints"I know cameras can be challenging—how can we see you're here?"C1 - Cross-Modal Intros
Offer alternatives"Camera, chat, reactions—show us you're engaged somehow"P1 - Digital Backchanneling
Create camera moments"Let's do cameras on for introductions, then you choose"A4 - Remote Spotlight
Celebrate all presence"Love seeing that active chat participation!"AA1 - Partner Check-ins
Reference shared agreement"Remember our presence pact—everyone shows up somehow"C2 - Shared Experience

Participation Imbalance

Warning Signs:

Low Energy/Flat Engagement

Understanding the Dynamic:

  • Energy affects everyone's ability to show presence
  • Multiple factors beyond just "engagement"
  • Community energy is co-created
Warning Signs:
  • Minimal responses
  • Slow to start
  • Visible fatigue
  • Flat affect

Quick Reminders

The 3 Presence Check:

  • Teaching: Can all access my instruction equally?
  • Social: Are students connecting across spaces?
  • Cognitive: Are ideas building together?

The Presence Partnership:

  • Instructors create opportunities
  • Students actively participate
  • Both negotiate balance together
If presence is breaking down → Check the Hybrid Presence Lens for diagnosis
